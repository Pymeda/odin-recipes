Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe will show you how to make the most exquisite and delicious chocolate chip cookies of your entire life that are to die for!!!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Brown sugar
  • Chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In one large bowl, mix in the flour, sugar, baking soda, and chocolate chips
  2. Once mixed in, pour in the melted butter, eggs, and vanilla extract
  3. Mix further untill the batter gets thicker and all ingredients are incorporated
  4. Refrigerate overnight
  5. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees Celsius
  6. Make cylindrical balls of cookie dough using an ice cream scoop
  7. Place the shaped dough onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper
  8. Cook for 8-10 minutes or untill the edges of the cookies are slightly brown and the inner section is golden
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool on a cooling rack for 15 minutes
